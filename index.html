Thommy Food Hub Digital Ad Campaign

I utilized dynamic motion graphics to bring the brand's playful mascot and vibrant menu to life. By compositing product shots with dynamic liquid splashes and 3D-style floating ingredients, the video creates an immersive visual experience.

Motion Graphics After Effects Video Editing

"Magtanim" - Geometric Digital Art

This piece reimagines a familiar scene of local farmers at work through a contemporary geometric lens. By breaking down the figures and the landscape into sharp, colorful triangles, the artwork captures the dynamic energy and collective effort of planting.

Digital Art Geometric Design Illustration

Thommy Food Hub — "Bring Your Own Cup" Campaign

This energetic and eye-catching promotional poster was designed for Thommy Food Hub to encourage sustainability while driving sales. The campaign incentivizes eco-friendly behavior by offering a 10% discount to customers who bring their own cups.

Poster Design Graphic Design Branding

Thommy Food Hub — Product Lineup Poster

Complementing the high-energy "Bring Your Own Cup" campaign, this poster adopts a clean, minimalist aesthetic to highlight the brand's core product lineup and competitive pricing. The design focuses on clarity and appetite appeal, positioning the beverages as an accessible, everyday luxury at the ₱39 price point.
