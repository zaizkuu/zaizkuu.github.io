Featured Projects

A selection of my recent work

Thommy Food Hub Digital Ad Campaign

I utilized dynamic motion graphics to bring the brand's playful mascot and vibrant menu to life. By compositing product shots with dynamic liquid splashes and 3D-style floating ingredients, the video creates an immersive visual experience. Clean typography and rapid transitions ensure the flavors and accessible price point remained the focal call-to-action.

Motion Graphics After Effects Video Editing

"Magtanim" - Geometric Digital Art

This piece reimagines a familiar scene of local farmers at work through a contemporary geometric lens. By breaking down the figures and the landscape into sharp, colorful triangles, the artwork captures the dynamic energy and collective effort of planting. It is a striking blend of traditional heritage and modern digital design techniques, exploring how complex human motion can be conveyed through basic shapes.

Digital Art Geometric Design Illustration
